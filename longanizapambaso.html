Pambazo de Longaniza

Cost: $12

An original dish that everyone in the restaurant wishes to eat for being a food to eat at festivals and parties in Mexico. While mass producing such a food is insane work for those with a passion for cooking, everyone eating appretiates the tangy and spicy flavor of longaniza with diced potatoes along with the flare of salsa, lettuce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Longaniza
  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Salsa Verde
